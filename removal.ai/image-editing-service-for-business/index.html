Choosing the Right Image Editing Service for Your Business

Yen Pedrajas October 22, 2023

Images are essential, but editing to enhance their quality and visual appeal is also critical. Before publishing photos for the public eye, it is important to do some photo retouching based on the project you are working on. Statistics-wise, around 40% of the content is most likely to resonate in the digital world and gain more audiences if you have outstanding visual materials. Besides, Simply Measured emphasized that you can enjoy a 65% increase in image engagement and a 46% boost in content clicks for brands if you practice photo retouching through image editing services.

Yet, in order to reap the rewards of having grabbing visuals and the display of brand elements, it is still a must to ensure that the photos you release are factual and free of errors if you are in a news outlet, branded content, or any formal business. It is also worth noting that, with respect to content that is anchored in reality, photo editing can enhance clarity and highlight substantial details. On the other hand, you have the creative license to go all-out and play with your imagination when manipulating images if you are into advertising and marketing to grab customer’s attention.

Different Types of Image Editing Services

Nowadays, you can explore photo editing through different platforms and image editing services that offer features you can use when you are planning to make brochures, magazines, infographics, and social media posts. Therefore, you have a wide array of choices when it comes to selecting the best platforms where you can level up your image editing, play with different types of photo services, and uphold your brand while captivating your audiences.

Given that you know how vital photographs and the process of image editing are, then, you have to understand that there are several types of image editing services that you can do or avail once you have some projects that heavily deal with this kind of content.

  • Background Removal

It is evident that many industries are fond of background extraction or background removal for photos. This process is widely employed in graphic design, advertising, and social media content to deliver better outputs for the audience. There are many available online image editing platforms that everyone can use to remove backgrounds with a few clicks. With these automation tools, you can erase and change the photo’s backgrounds immediately, free from the hassle and inconvenience of a manual process. You can check out Removal.AI and many more if you want to have free tools and acquire premium features that can upgrade your editing journey.

  • Image resizing

This type of image editing service is a common and crucial task in image processing and enhancement. This part involves changing the dimensions, such as the width and height, of an image while maintaining its aspect ratio or modifying it if needed. But you must remember that resizing an image must not decrease the quality of the content. So, always be careful when doing this to avoid compromising the image resolution. In the graphics design industry, many of them opt to shrink large pictures to easily email or share them digitally, preserve aspect ratios, and downsize and upsize images. There are also many pieces of software that you can use to do this, like Photoshop, Canva, and Image Resizer.

  • Retouching

When you modify digital images to improve their appearance or correct any technical issues, it’s called photo retouching or picture editing. Resizing, adjusting color and shape, and removing defects are some of the essential techniques of this process. Besides, photo retouching is a method of enhancing an image for presentation. Unlike editing, retouching involves advanced skills and techniques to transform or alter a picture to a greater extent. One example of portrait retouching is skin smoothing. It is commonly performed using image editing software like Adobe Photoshop or GIMP. Overall, retouching is a creative way to enhance digital images, remove imperfections through quality restoration and creative manipulation, and make them look more attractive and professional.

  • Ghost Mannequin

If you’re running an online clothing store, you might want to showcase your products in an attractive and professional way. One photo editing technique that is commonly used in the e-commerce industry is called ghost mannequin, which is also known as invisible mannequin or hollow man effect. This technique allows you to present your garments without using an actual mannequin or model. This is common to see in social media posts, like on Instagram, Facebook, and online shopping sites. Traditionally, you can also see this in most fashion magazines or catalogs. This type of image editing service is an effective technique because it highlights the design and fit of your clothing item without any distractions from a visible mannequin or model. Generally, ghost mannequins are a popular method used by clothing businesses in a professional and visually appealing way. By using this technique, you can ensure that your products stand out and grab the attention of potential customers.

  • Color Correction

When it comes to visual media industries like photography, filmmaking, video production, and graphic design, color correction, also called color grading, is a vital process. This technique involves adjusting and enhancing the colors of an image or video to achieve a specific aesthetic or to correct any color-related problems. The primary goal of color correction is to make sure that the output accurately represents the original scene or subject as seen by the human eye. It means manipulating the colors of an image or video to create visual consistency across different shots and scenes and fix any color imbalances or errors introduced during capture or post-production. Along the way, you need to balance the colors to adjust the overall color balance and ensure the white balance is correct. Secondly, exposure correction involves adjusting the brightness and contrast levels to achieve the right amount of detail while balancing highlights and shadows. Software tools like Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom, Premiere Pro, and Davinci Resolve, among others, are commonly used for this purpose. With color correction, you can transform your images or videos into something more visually appealing, and that’s the beauty of this process.

Understanding Your Business Needs

Now is the moment when you will connect certain photography styles and techniques to your business to make use of your knowledge and expertise. It is important to narrow down what your business needs to efficiently target your market.

  • Portrait and Model Photography

Portrait and model photography are two types of photography that focus on capturing images of individuals, highlighting their unique characteristics, personalities, and expressions. Although these genres share similarities, they also have distinct differences. Portrait photography is the art of capturing the likeness, personality, and essence of a person or a small group. The main goal is to emphasize the subject’s facial features, emotions, and sometimes their surroundings. This genre aims to establish a connection between the viewer and the subject by evoking emotions and telling a story. You can do some environmental, studio, and candid portraits.

On the other hand, model photography focuses on photographing professional or amateur models to showcase clothing, accessories, makeup, or other fashion and beauty products. The primary purpose is to promote products, brands, or styles in industries like fashion, beauty, advertising, and editorial. While portrait and model photography serve different purposes, employ distinct techniques, and target diverse audiences, both rely on the photographer’s expertise to bring out the best in their subjects and create captivating images. These genres often involve collaboration between the photographer and the subject or model to achieve the desired outcomes.

  • E-Commerce

E-commerce photography refers to the practice of creating professional photographs of products that are intended to be sold online through e-commerce platforms or websites. In the context of online shopping, product images play a crucial role in attracting potential customers and influencing their purchasing decisions. It is critical since it enables consumers to relate to your brand and goods through visual materials. For online businesses, efficient e-commerce picture editing is essential since attractive product images may greatly boost client engagement and conversion rates. Online competition is strong, so it takes exceptional e-commerce photography to stand out.

You can do this through product presentations through images and elements, lighting, a backdrop, and consistency in colors. Overall, E-commerce photography is an integral part of online retailing, and investing in high-quality product images can lead to increased sales and customer satisfaction. As the online shopping industry continues to grow, the importance of well-executed e-commerce photography becomes even more pronounced.

  • Real Estate

Moreover, another business that you can focus on is real estate. Real estate photography is a specialized form of photography focused on capturing images of properties for the purpose of marketing and selling them. It involves taking high-quality photographs of residential and commercial properties, including houses, apartments, condos, office spaces, and more.

The objective of real estate photography is to highlight the place or buildings in the best possible light, emphasizing its vital features, designs, architecture, and overall ambiance. These photographs are then used by real estate agents, property owners, and developers to create visually appealing listings for advertising and promotional purposes. Here, you have to focus on composition, lighting, exterior and interior angles, and the overall look of the photos.

The essence of high-quality real estate photography cannot be understated, as it significantly influences potential buyers’ first impressions and can lead to increased interest and quicker property sales. In the competitive real estate market, visually appealing images can make a significant difference in attracting potential buyers and closing deals.

  • Apparel Business

Another niche that leverages image editing services for their business is clothing photography. This industry aims to practice capturing high-quality images of clothing, accessories, and other fashion items for commercial purposes. The main goal of apparel business photography is to showcase the products in a visually top-notch and eye-candy approach to captivate prospective customers and boost sales.

In this kind of business, brand marketing and advertising are essential because your consumers’ decisions will be based on how you present your products in relation to your business’s reputation and trademark.

  • Others

Undeniably, there are way more businesses that zero in on image editing services, photo retouching, and upgrading their visual assets. Whether you are in the startup, equipment, technology, arts, or medical fields, you will surely have to familiarize yourself with or master editing and boosting your publicity materials and image-related content to connect with your audiences and consumers.

Choosing the Right Service Provider

Choosing the right service provider is a priority to guarantee the success and satisfaction of the people you are serving. This is the initial and important step before you dive into the photography tools that you will be using, whether on a short- or long-term subscription. So, you better keep the following points in mind when selecting the right image editing service provider for your business:

Reputation and Reviews: Always check and verify testimonials from previous users, experts, or reputable reviewers in the field. Go to different online platforms, such as social media and industry-specific sites, to comprehensively gauge the reputation of the service provider you are eyeing. Positive feedback and high ratings are indicators of reliability and quality, so always go for that. Additionally, you can always request a portfolio or case studies of the service provider’s previous work if you are dealing with people or photo editors. This will give you an idea of the projects they have worked on and their ability to deliver results.

Experience and Expertise: Examine the service provider’s experience in the field. If it is a tool, always consider how long it has been in the industry, how many people are using it, whether it is adaptive to changes and responsive to people’s needs, and always track its record to ensure reliability. Experienced providers are more likely to handle changes effectively and provide high-quality solutions.

Cost and Value: Consider the cost of the services in relation to the value they provide. Do not automatically go for the cheapest option; focus on finding a tool that offers a fair balance between cost and quality. There are many platforms and people that can help you address your concerns while still having affordable and reasonable rates.

Communication and Support: Healthy and immediate communication is a must for a successful collaboration. Hence, you should always evaluate how responsive and accessible the service provider is and ensure that they have clear communication channels and provide timely support. After-sales service is very important because the business does not stop after you purchase the product; it begins once you are actually in the phase of using it.

Remember, each service provider is unique, and the importance of these factors may vary depending on your specific requirements. Take your time, do thorough research, and make an informed decision to select the right service provider for your needs.
