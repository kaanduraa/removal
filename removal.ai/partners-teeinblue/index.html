Make POD Better With AI! Removal.AI & Teeinblue Partnership

Yen Pedrajas September 8, 2022

We’re excited to announce a new and exciting partnership with an awesome Print-On-Demand service–Teeinblue.

Teeinblue is an all-in-one product personalizer for Print-On-Demand businesses on Shopify. The software is built to help online merchants offer high-converting personalized products with live previews and automate the product personalization business.

With this new integration, sellers, especially POD merchants, can now use the “Remove background feature” on “Custom Photo Upload” products right in the Teeinblue app FOR FREE. Please view the demo product here.

More of Teeinblue’s main features:

  • 4 Personalized Options: Besides the custom photo upload option, you can sell custom text, custom clipart, and custom maps to your audience.
  • Design tool: The Photoshop-like editor makes it easier for you to create stunning personalized designs with multiple templates.
  • Add POD products: You can add product information from any Print-On-Demand provider.
  • Live Preview: Showing real-time updates on the mockup image (product gallery) according to users’ selection.
  • Order process: Keeping track of the order status, viewing design images, and most importantly, sending orders to your POD fulfillment service, including Ready-to-Print files.
