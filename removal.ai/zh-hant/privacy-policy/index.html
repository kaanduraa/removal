HOW DO WE USE UPLOADED IMAGES?

100% confidentially

All images that are uploaded on our site is secured and treated with utmost confidentiality

Encrypted transferring tunnel

The transfer process is through SSL/TLS-encrypted data transfer and will only be used in the process of removing the background. You will then be offered to download the results.

No sharing

Images uploaded and those that have their backgrounds removed will not be shared to third parties. We also do not publish or do any further actions to them.

None of your images stay with us

We delete both the uploaded image and the result image an hour after the upload at the latest.

Apart from the said uses, we also keep usage statistics (sans images). We also receive personal data if you will contact us by email. The personal data received will likewise be used by our team to reply to you. Data is also used for bookkeeping purposes if we have established a customer/supplier relationship.
